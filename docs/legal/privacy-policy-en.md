## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Lawh ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Lawh, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 2. Our Privacy Principles

Lawh is built with privacy as a core principle:

- **No Personal Accounts Required:** We use device-based identification, not personal accounts with names, emails, or passwords
- **Minimal Data Collection:** We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our services
- **No Data Selling:** We never sell your personal data to third parties
- **Transparency:** We clearly explain what data we collect and why
- **User Control:** You have control over optional data collection features

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

**Device Information:**
- Device unique identifier (UUID) - a random identifier generated for your device, not linked to your personal identity
- Device type (iOS or Android)
- App version

**Usage Data:**
- Number of searches performed (daily and monthly counts)
- Search timestamps
- Session information (when you open and use the App)

**Technical Data:**
- Processing times for search requests
- Error logs for troubleshooting

### 3.2 Information You Provide

When you use our services, you may provide:

**Audio Recordings:**
- Voice recordings of Quranic recitations that you submit for verse identification
- These recordings are processed to extract text and are not stored permanently

**Feedback Data:**
- Thumbs up/down ratings on search results
- Verse corrections when results are inaccurate
- This helps us improve our matching accuracy

### 3.3 Information Collected with Your Consent

With your explicit permission, we may collect:

**Location Data (Optional):**
- GPS coordinates (if you grant location permission)
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- City, region, and country information

You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The App functions fully without location data.

### 3.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

- Names or personal identifiers
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Passwords or login credentials
- Contacts or address book data
- Photos or media files (except audio you submit for search)
- Browsing history
- Financial information (handled by Apple/Google)
- Biometric data

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Data Storage

- Your data is stored on secure cloud servers
- Device identifiers and usage data are retained as long as you use the App
- Audio recordings are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- Search history is maintained for your convenience

### 6.2 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Device identifier | Until App uninstallation or account deletion request |
| Search history | Duration of App usage |
| Audio recordings | Processed immediately, not stored |
| Subscription data | Duration of subscription + legal requirements |
| Analytics data | Aggregated and anonymized over time |

### 6.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure server infrastructure
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
